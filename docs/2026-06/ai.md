# Fake Support Scam Detection & Prevention Checklist

## Quick Identification Checklist

### Red Flags for Fake Customer Service
- [ ] Unsolicited contact (SMS, phone, messaging app) claiming account issues
- [ ] Pressure to click links or provide verification codes immediately
- [ ] Requests for passwords, OTP, or card details
- [ ] Generic greetings (e.g., "尊敬的用户" vs. your actual name/order number)
- [ ] Typos or grammatical errors in official communications
- [ ] Links that don't match official domain (e.g., taobao-help.fake vs. taobao.com)

### AI Deepfake & Spoofing Signs
- [ ] Video call with unnatural blinking, lip-sync delays, or static background
- [ ] Audio quality inconsistencies or robotic pauses
- [ ] Phone number shows caller ID but voice doesn't match official channels
- [ ] Request to turn on camera "for security verification"

## Platform-Specific Verification Methods

### Taobao/Alipay
**Official Contact Channels:**
- In-app message center only (never click external links)
- Official phone: 95188 (Taobao) / 95188 (Alipay) — verify by calling back using number on official website
- Never share verification codes sent to phone

**Red Flag:** Links to fake Taobao login pages (check domain: alipay.com vs. alipay.fake)

### Banking/Payment Apps
**Official Contact Channels:**
- Bank's official app notifications only
- Official customer service phone (look up on bank website, don't use provided number)
- Visit branch in person for account issues

**Red Flag:** Bank will never ask for PIN, passwords, or OTP codes via call/SMS/email

### Courier/Logistics
**Official Contact Channels:**
- Company official app or website tracking
- Phone number on package label (verify independently)
- WeChat official account (check blue verification badge)

**Red Flag:** "Customs fee pending" or "delivery failed" messages with payment links

## Defense Protocol

1. **Hang Up & Call Back:** Never use phone number provided in unsolicited call. Look up official number on company website.
2. **Verify in Official Channels:** Log into app/website directly (not via provided link) to check account status.
3. **Never Share:** OTP, verification codes, passwords, card numbers under any circumstance.
4. **Report:** Screenshot and report to official platform immediately if suspected fraud.
5. **Verify Before Payment:** Never pay via wire transfer, gift cards, or untraceable methods based on customer service claims.
